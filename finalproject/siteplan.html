Name: The Game Database

Purpose

To explore existing titles within a defined user’s Steam Library and allow them to explore and discover new titles that they may be interested in. This can be accomplished by filtering the existing library to discover details about items or by searching genres of new titles.

Scenarios

What is the top-played game in my library? What other ‘Platformers’ (filtered genre search) might I be interested in?
